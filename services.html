Food programs

Anyone is allowed to take food as they need from the main garden. There is also a food pantry store of harvested foods that is available upon request (call or email the food team for more information).

Garden with the garden team

The garden team hosts gardening hours to teach about growing different plants as well as help you with any gardening projects you're interested in (call or email the garden team for more information).

Garden parties

Every month, the events team hosts a potluck party where members can bring food prepared from garden produce and make new friends (call or email the events team for more information).

Reserve the garden

If you would like to reserve the garden for an event for your school, club, youth group, etc., call or email the events team.
